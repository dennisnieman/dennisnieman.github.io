about

I'm a PhD student in mathematical statistics at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. My supervisors are Harry van Zanten and Botond Szabo.

I work on the frequentist validity of variational Bayesian methods. I'm also working on a publication based on my master thesis on statistics in a Lévy process queueing model.
